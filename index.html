Child Safety Standards Policy

JPSS NGO – Community Support

Developer: Jeevana Pragati Seva Samste

At JPSS NGO – Community Support, we are committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe platform for all users. We have a strict zero-tolerance policy against any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

We strictly prohibit any content or behavior related to:
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Child exploitation
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming or solicitation of minors
  • Sharing or promoting abusive content involving children

Any violation will result in immediate removal of the content and permanent suspension of the user account.

2. User Reporting System

Users can report harmful or inappropriate content directly within the application. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team and appropriate action is taken immediately.

3. Content Moderation

JPSS NGO actively monitors and moderates content shared on the platform to ensure compliance with our policies. Content that violates child safety guidelines will be removed without notice.

4. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

If we identify any content related to child sexual abuse or exploitation, we will report it to relevant law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations.

5. Child Safety Contact

If you encounter content that may violate child safety policies, please contact us immediately.

  • Organization: Jeevana Pragati Seva Samste
  • App Name: JPSS NGO – Community Support
  • Email: support@jpssngo.org
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours

6. Compliance

Our application complies with:

  • Google Play Child Safety Standards Policy
  • International child protection regulations
